Screening and Evaluation

Enhancing Efficiency: Strategies for Screening and Evaluation

  1. WEB APPLICATION

    Stage One

    The first step in entering the Branch Karma funding process is our online application. This process includes filling out an application with an overview of your company, including a summary of your financials. You’ll also upload an investor PowerPoint presentation and any additional materials you feel are relevant

  2. REVIEW

    Stage Two

    Branch Karma team members perform an initial screening of the application to ensure it is within our scope of interest. If you pass the initial review stage, you will be invited to present at an initial meeting.

  3. INITIAL MEETING

    Stage Three

    Each meeting process varies, but the general concept is an elevator pitch between 8 and 15 minutes, followed by Q&A. The prescreen presentation is given to our small team of Branch Karma intake coordinators who have expertise in your technology or market on a video call. The purpose of the initial meeting is to determine if you are ready to present to the larger group, and give you constructive feedback to improve your presentation.

    Presentations are usually recorded so that members who are not present can participate later online on our member-only meetings.

  4. MAIN MEETING

    Stage Four

    You’ll receive feedback if we feel you are the right fit and scope for the projects we engage in. The meeting will be recorded and you will be given additional questions and feedback. Once all questions and clarifications are satisfied you will be invited to have a detailed meeting with our expert team to discuss how a joint venture or collaboration can occur.

  5. DUE DILIGENCE

    Stage Five

    During this period we verify representations by speaking with customers, reviewing agreements and patents, checking references, backgrounds, etc. if the results are positive, the partnership moves forward to funding.

  6. FUNDING

    Stage Six

    The final stage of disbursement. Once all metrics have been satisfied. Now we work towards expansion.
